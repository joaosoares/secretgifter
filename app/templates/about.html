Hello! My name is Jo√£o Soares and I am the founder of SecretGifter.

This web application started just before Easter 2013. As of custom, my friends and I were planning a secret gift exchange, or Secret Santa, to celebrate the festivities.

However, this year, we wouldn't be able to do the whole "draw-name-from-paper" ritual that preceeds a typical Secret Santa.

Because of our inability to gather beforehand, I offered to write a small python script that would include everyone's name, randomly pair them up, and send a secret SMS to each participant.

Since then, I started developing this service as a way to give that functionality to everyone else on the Internet. This website uses the service from the awesome Twilio, so I have to charge for text messages.

I hope you enjoy it!
